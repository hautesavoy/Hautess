Skepta shuts down Toronto's Rebel Nightclub

On the steady rise to global prominence, it isn’t difficult to imagine the following the overseas artist has managed to accumulate in Toronto. The packed crowd could contain hold their excitement waiting for Skepta to take the stage. With devote fans bellowing his intricate lyrics, the UK grime artist provided a performance precisely congruent with his aggressive, and unapologetic sound. Matching this aggression were the innocuous shoves amongst members of the audience willing to mosh. With special appearances by Riff-Raff, and Toronto’s very own Smoke Dawg, the night will be one to remember.



As the second last stop of his Banned in America Tour, Skepta isn’t showing any signs of slowing down. Following his debut appearance at Coachella Music Festival, the artist is slated to perform at the Governors Ball in New York, and Bonnaroo in Tennessee—be on the look out.
