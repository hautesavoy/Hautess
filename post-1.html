Sweet. Sexy. Samaritan.

Kehlani’s performance at Toronto’s Rebel Nightclub was dazzling. Its difficult to imagine anyone could avoid becoming enamored of the young superstar. While she refers to herself as timid, on stage, Kehlani appeared indomitable, and larger than life. However, despite her palpable confidence, Kehlani’s on stage persona is redolent of an adolescent friend. Her empathetic temperament, coupled with constant quips, and questions to the crowd, formed a sense of community, and shared identity amongst audience members. She frequently paused between tracks to engage the audience, and share personal—yet relatable— anecdotes, and feelings behind particular tracks. Consequently, nearly every lyric of each song felt as though its had been written for you. Many attendees were left imbued with self-assurance, and empathy. The positive environment, which Kehlani had established, further emphasized her familiarity, and was replicated by audience members; many stopping to alert security of fainting fans, or sharing water that Kehlani provided.
