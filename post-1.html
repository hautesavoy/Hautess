Desiigner lights up Toronto's Phoenix Concert Theatre

Desiigner’s performance at the Phoenix Concert Theatre was therapeutic. Immediately upon entering the stage, the youthful performers energy and charisma could be felt. This, coupled with his trademark milly-rock, and spastic dabs left the audience more than amused. While Desiigner is well known for performing with great alacrity, a few members of the audience weren’t sure what to expect. One individual proclaims:


“I knew it would be lit, but this was something else”

However, Desiigner isn’t the only member of his posse bringing the heat. Both hype-man Milly, and DJ Rellyrell matched Desiigner’s palpable energy amplifying the crowd every record. Milly controlled the audience, adding instructive ad-libs, directing the crowd’s movements between sets. Similarly, DJ RellyRell provided a pre-show set worthy of its own show. This performance marked the last stop on Desiigner’s Outlet Tour, which started in April. With an album in the works, there is no doubt, Desiigner will be infiltrating our speakers throughout the summer.
