Soul searching never felt better

Ab-Soul is a performer like none other. His sense of calm and assurance permeated the venue once he appeared on stage. Along side hype-man Doe Burger, the two managed to provide the perfect atmosphere for audience members to vibe to. The TDE artist took the road in April for his YMF tour, promoting his LP Do What Thou Wilt.

With lyrical substance, and his characteristic mysticism, Ab-Soul exhibits mastery within his class like none other. The night served as a reminder of the artist’s dedication, and progression. Performing hits like Terrorist Threats, Illuminate, Pineal Gland, and D.R.U.G.S, Ab-Soul unknowingly displayed his ability to adapt, whilst bringing his signature style.
